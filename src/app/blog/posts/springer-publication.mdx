## Abstract

Communication is a fundamental part of the requirements elicitation process for a software project. Constant changes in requirements make it difficult to complete projects successfully and meet customer expectations. This chapter analyzes the key communication challenges between clients and developers, and proposes strategies to improve the elicitation process.
